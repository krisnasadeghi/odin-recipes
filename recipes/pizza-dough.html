Pizza Dough

The following recipe yields enough dough for three standard size pizza bases

Ingredients

  • 600 grams plain flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 7 grams yeast
  • 1 and 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • pinch of sugar

Method

  1. Add the yeast to the warm water. Add sugar and mix. Wait until the mixture become frothy (about 5 minutes).
  2. Add the yeast mixture to the flour along with the salt and olive oil. Use a knife to mix the ingredients using a slicing motion.
  3. Roll the dough out onto a floured chopping board and knead until the ball of dough springs back when you push on it with your finger.
  4. Divide the mixture into three portions. Roll out immediately or freeze.
